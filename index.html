The Approach

From "I don't know" to a decision you both trust — in 4 sessions

No generic aptitude tests, no one-size-fits-all lists. A structured process built around who your child actually is.

01

Discover

An in-depth conversation plus an internationally certified psychometric assessment, mapping real strengths, interests and personality — not guesses.

02

Reflect

A guided session unpacking the results together, so the insight belongs to your child, not just a report they'll never re-read.

03

Explore

Matching that self-knowledge against real streams, courses and careers — including strong options nobody had suggested yet.

04

Decide

A joint parent-student session to land on a direction the whole family understands, trusts and actually supports.
